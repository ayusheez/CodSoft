Overview of the Internship Program

Our Web Developer Internship Program at CodSoft offers aspiring developers the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in the field of web development. This program runs for a duration of 12 weeks and is designed to provide you with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the tech industry.

Highlights and Benefits

  • Real-world experience on live projects
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals
  • Opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies
  • Networking opportunities within the tech community
  • Potential for full-time employment upon successful completion

Qualifications and Desired Skills

  • Basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Familiarity with front-end frameworks such as React or Angular
  • Knowledge of back-end technologies such as Node.js or Python
  • Strong problem-solving skills and a willingness to learn
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
